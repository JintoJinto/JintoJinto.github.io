WHY UX?

I love creating seamless and intuitive digital experience that make people's life easier.



I have a passion for exploration. Exploring new ideas and craft them to see positive impact on people. This is why I fall in love with UX design.
My insatiable curiosity and bi-cultural background help me explore new ways to solve issues.

A passion for creating.

Flexible and adaptable.

Storytelling.

Management and team work.
