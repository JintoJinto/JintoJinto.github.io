WHY UX?

Hi, I'm Estelle a French Chinese UX designer based in London

I have a passion for exploration

Exploring new ideas and craft them to see positive impact on people. This is why I fall in love with UX design.

My eclectic background and curious mind help me find different ways to solve issues, small or big.

I love creating seamless and intuitive digital experience that make people's life easier.

My insatiable curiosity and bi-cultural background help me explore new ways to solve issues.

After a Master degree in Business Management, I spent the past 10 years in Shanghai working and traveling across Asia.

From my different experiences, I gained a unique set of skills. A passion for creating.

I started my career in Marketing, launching and developing new beauty products for SEPHORA brand. Flexible and adaptable.

Fascinated by new technologies, I joined in 2009 an E-commerce startup, MEI.com (now Alibaba group), as part of the initial merchandising team. Storytelling.

Later, I learned the power of good story telling while working as a Talent Agent for international TVC directors. Management, team work and presentation skills.

My recent experience as a digital account manager for a creative branding agency, taught me invaluable presentation skills.

All these "iterations" led me organically to the world of UX design.

Newly graduated from General Assembly, I'm now looking for opportunities to join an inspiring team!

I'm particularly interested in education, health and travel sectors. Want to grab a coffee?
